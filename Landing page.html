About us

Established in the year 1971 the brand Chandana Brothers was founded by Mr. Chandana Mohan Rao; and Director Mr. Mavuri Venkata Ramana has introduced the Brand CMR Shopping Mall. They changed the Shopping pattern in Andhra Pradesh by starting CMR Shopping Mall.

Features

  • It is an enclosed shopping space.
  • It is large multi-storeyed air-conditioned building.
  • It has shops on different floors.
  • It also consists of restaurants and even a cinema theatre.
  • It sells both branded and non-branded goods.
